Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 April 2026

This privacy policy explains how Stevenson Marketing Limited ("we", "us", "our"), trading as CallFirst, collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our website at callfirst.co.uk and our services.

1. Who We Are

Stevenson Marketing Limited is a company registered in England and Wales. We operate the CallFirst AI-powered lead generation platform for UK businesses. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at hello@callfirst.co.uk.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data:

  • Customer enquiry data: Name, phone number, job details, property type, and preferred callback time — collected through our AI intake agent on client websites.
  • Client data: Business name, contact name, email address, phone number, trade, and area — collected during onboarding.
  • Website visitor data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referral source — collected via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Communication data: SMS and WhatsApp messages sent and received through our platform.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use personal data for the following purposes:

  • To deliver leads and job briefs to our clients (trade businesses).
  • To send SMS or WhatsApp confirmations and follow-up messages to customers on behalf of our clients.
  • To manage client accounts, billing, and support.
  • To improve our AI intake agent and platform performance.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data on the following legal bases under UK GDPR:

  • Legitimate interest: Processing customer enquiries and delivering leads to the relevant trade business.
  • Contract: Providing our services to clients who have signed up for CallFirst.
  • Consent: Where a customer voluntarily provides their details through the AI intake agent.
  • Legal obligation: Where required by law.

5. Who We Share Data With

We share personal data with:

  • Our clients: The trade business that the customer is enquiring about receives the customer's name, phone number, job details, and callback preferences.
  • Service providers: We use third-party services including Twilio (SMS/WhatsApp messaging), Anthropic (AI processing), Neon (database hosting), Clerk (authentication), Stripe (payments), and Railway (hosting). Each processes data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

6. Data Retention

Customer enquiry data is retained for up to 24 months after the enquiry date, after which it is anonymised or deleted. Client account data is retained for the duration of the contract and for 6 years afterwards for accounting purposes.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Request restriction of processing.
  • Data portability.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@callfirst.co.uk.

8. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies required for the site to function. We may also use analytics cookies to understand how visitors use the site. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data, including encrypted data transmission (TLS), secure database hosting, access controls, and regular security reviews.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will be amended accordingly. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
